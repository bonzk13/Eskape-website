About Us

EsKape is your coffee sanctuary on campus, a place where students, teachers, and coffee lovers gather for a perfect cup and inspiration. We believe in quality, creativity, and providing an inviting space for everyone to enjoy. Our mission is to foster community, creativity, and connection through the love of coffee.
